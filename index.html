The Philippine Gallery



The Philippines is a beautiful archipelagic country found in Southeast Asia composed of roughly 7,641 islands.

With so many islands, there are so many places to travel, explore, and see the Filipino culture at its finest!



Three Destinations in The Philippines You Must Travel To!



Boracay

Located in the Western Visayas region, Boracay is a small island with beautiful sceneries on the beaches of both sides. The White Beach located along the west coast have palm trees, bars and restaurants for a laid back experience while the Bulabog Beach on the east coast have strong winds for those looking to do water sports.

Siargao

Known as the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines," the Siargao Island is well defined by it's tear-drop shape. It features stunning beaches, lagoons, rock pools, and the famous Cloud 9 surfing spot. If you are to go here, prepare for activities such as island hopping, snorkeling, and diving.

Baguio

Baguio is known as the summer capital of the Philippines and the "City of Pines" and for good reason. The unusually colder weather makes it a popular hotspot for many ongoers during the summer. Locations such as the Valley of Colors makes Baguio a great escape from the heavily urbanized areas in the Philippines.
